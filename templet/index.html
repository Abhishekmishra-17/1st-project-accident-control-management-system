About Accident Control Management System

Abstract:

In the Whole word road accidents are increasing day by day. Many peoples are injured and lost their life in accident. There are many causes by which the accidents can occurred. This project is about all possible solutions of causes of accidents. This project is working on both hardware as well as software.

Introduction:

As the name suggest "Accident Control Management System", it controls the accidents as much as possible. The model is based on Image Processing, Internet of Things,Nural Networks and some other supportive technologies. Before we discussed about solution, let's looks a mintue on causes of accident.
